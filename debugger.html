Rapid Shoot [Rapid Fire]

For 60 seconds, bullet bow Normal Attack fires three shots instead of one, in exchange for lower damage per shot and slower interval. The skill has 110 seconds cooldown.

This is an “Active Skill”. Put it on your subpalette and activate it, just like how you use skills in other MMO games. It serves two purposes: faster PP Recovery and temporary damage boost. Firing three arrows means that you gain triple the amount of PP back and learning extra skill related to Rapid Shoot grants you a massive amount of range damage increase.

Remember to use it when it is ready. Otherwise, hold if necessary in certain situations. Rapid Shoot always has around one second activation animation, so you never get the full duration regardless of reasons. If you are flinched during it, you will lose the skill effect. However, switching to any weapons does not deactivate the skill.

The following skills further enhance Rapid Shoot.

  • Rapid Shoot Up 1 & 2 [Rapid Fire Up 1 & 2]: During Rapid Shoot, gain up to +500 R-ATK while equipping Bullet Bow. “Rapid Shoot Up 1” gives up to 200 R-ATK while the “Rapid Shoot Up 2”, however, gives up to 300 R-ATK. Both skills stack additively. They are added after stats calculation from enhancements like Shifta and Team ATK buff.
  • Rapid Shoot Mastery [Rapid Fire Mastery]: Gain damage bonus while equipped with Bullet Bow and Rapid Shoot is active.
  • Rapid Shoot Advance [Advanced Rapid Fire]: Reduce the Rapid Shoot’s cooldown and increase the amount of PP recovered from normal attacks. It reduces Rapid Shoot cooldown to 77 seconds from 110 seconds, resulting in 17 seconds downtime or less. The PP recovery boost is also significant, and combining with other bonuses means a charged Normal Attack recovers at least half of your PP bar.

There’s a trick you can do. If you properly time your attack when Rapid Shoot expires, you should be firing triple arrows with the damage equivalent to your regular charged Normal Attack. It takes time to be consistent.

Charge Shoot [Charged Shot]: With Bullet Bow equipped, your charged normal attack deals more damage and its projectile travels faster. It doesn’t turn your arrows into Assault Rifle’s bullet, but hitting the enemies quicker means the damage and PP recovery occurs faster.

Bullet Bow Charge Bonus [Bow Charge Bonus]: (MAIN CLASS ONLY) When equipped with Bullet Bow, your chargeable PA has reduced charge time and PP consumption. It reduces the PP Consumption of those PA by 5, applying AFTER any percentage reduction from other sources. Besides, charge time reduction is noticeable the more you play with the weapon.
