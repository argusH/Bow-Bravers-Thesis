EP5 (EN)

I wasn’t that active during the EP5 era in the EN server. However, thanks to the generosity of the people behind the game, I finally have some gears to work my way around. Atlas Agria Ex with ghetto affixes, 5 slots Trailblaze and Cleasis units, and Fornis Reng (thanks, my JP friends) were enough for me.

The palette setup and MAG setting aren’t that important here. As long as you find your preference and you have level 200 DEX MAG, it’s definitely fine.
